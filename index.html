About Me

I am a natural born coder with a love for beautifully written code. My main focus has been back-end development in C# on the .NET platform. Lately my focus has been Web APIs, but I also have experience with MVC and Web Forms.. ..but if someone mentions Web Forms I'm very likely to run away screaming with my arms panickingly flailing.

I’m also a member of Mensa since 1998, don't hang out with them much though, but I quite enjoy their opera nights.
